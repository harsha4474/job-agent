How it works

From job boards to
your inbox, automatically.

Every evening the agent wakes up, does the work, and sends you only what matters.

1
Searches 3 job boards simultaneously
Pulls listings from JSearch (LinkedIn, Indeed, ZipRecruiter, Glassdoor), Remotive, and Jobicy — filtered to PM roles only.
node-cron · 6 PM EST daily
2
Deduplicates across all previous runs
Tracks every job ever seen in a persistent file. The same listing will never appear in your digest twice.
seen_jobs.json · persistent storage
3
Scores every listing with GPT-4o
Each job is scored 0–100 against the candidate resume. GPT-4o understands context — not just keyword overlap.
OpenAI GPT-4o · batch scoring
4
Updates Google Sheet + sends email digest
Top 50 jobs appended to a tracker with status dropdowns. Email digest sent with apply links and match scores.
Google Sheets API · SendGrid
